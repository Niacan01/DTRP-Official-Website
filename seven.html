Physical Description

Seven has a short build and a very thin frame, due to a lack of natural sunlight, Seven's skin is extremely light. Seven has short light yellow and baby pink hair and large silver eyes. Seven is commonly seen wearing a white shirt and white pleated skirt with tall white rain boots.

Seven has a mask that fits over their facial features, it is white with a black open smile on it. They are never seen without their mask.

Personality

Note:

Seven's body is the host of several different entities. Due to this, this section will focus on primarily SEVEN's personality, and not the others.

Seven has a very unstable personality at times but is generally known to be very curious and have a high sympathy for others, some of their behavior seems animalistic at times due to several past experiments.
