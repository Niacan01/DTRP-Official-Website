Summary

Physical Description

He has raven colored hair which is quite long, like sometimes to his neck, or shoulders if he forgets to cut it. Muscular slightly. Pale skin, gray eyes, used to be blue, a yellow triangle around his right eye and half of a white Pearl over his left eye. He wears long sleeves and long pants, clothes usually black, covering his scars from the castle, he has a round nose. He has an ancient sword (the Arnicicia sword, a sword that can kill the gods and deflect any spell) strapped to his right side, and a teleportation necklace on his neck.

Personality

Stubborn, smart, quick, sweet, strong, emotional, anxious at times, clingy when it comes to friends, overprotective and sometimes sarcastic. Antisocial and observant, quite obsessive. Feels weak, feels like he needs to be the strong one.

Backstory

When Zach was eight years old, Them's second in command, Falécia, appeared to Zach and tricked him into a deal. They shook hands and she knocked him out. When Zach woke up, his parents and twin sister had been murdered and his house was destroyed. Falécia kidnapped Zach to be trained as a general in the castle she served at.

At the castle, Zach was horribly abused, being starved and beaten into submission. He was placed as the leader of the Thirty Scouts. (30 children kidnapped similarly to Zach) Eventually, Zach befriended the 18th scout, Keira, when she helped him up after a fall.

Zach was told by Them that Mattie was part of a prophecy that would destroy the earth. If Zach managed to work for seven years and fulfil the prophecy, his parents would be brought back.

By the time Zach was ten, he managed to finally capture Mattie in a playground dimension. Mattie accidentally let out important information about her family and friends. Zach pretended to not care and "accidentally" let a portal open for her to escape.

Main Story

Zach first officially met the group in the spa dimension where he attempted to kidnap Mattie. Ruby and Zhen chased him down, eventually getting Mattie back.

Zach went on to attack the people Mattie mentioned, with Mattie trying to stop him. Eventually, Zach captured Mattie in the school since she decided to sacrifice herself after overhearing a conversation. Zach then found out all of his work had been a giant lie. Them didn’t have the power or the will to revive his family. Mattie and Tess took him in and retaught him things he had forgotten during his time at the castle.

Relationships

Zandra (Twin Sister)

Zach and Zandra both had a good relationship growing up and he was devastated when Zandra died.

Despite Tess's warning, Zach ended up taking a deal with Seven to revive Zandra and was happy to see her again.

Keira

Keira and Zach met when they were young, Zach had fallen down the stairs and Keira helped him up. They became best friends but were separated when Zach left. Eventually, Zach led a mission to free Keira and the other 30 Scouts.

Mattie

Zach was tasked with capturing Mattie and giving her to Them for the prophecy. Zach ended up betraying Them and so Mattie ended up being his friend.

Tesseract

Zach met Tess around the time of him capturing Mattie the final time, they didn't fully meet until Zach joined her and Mattie int he Anime Dimension.

Zach sees Tess are his friend but gets annoyed with her a lot.

Seven

Zach met Seven when they kidnapped him to be brought to Error, he wasn't bothered by Seven despite Seven's attempts to scare him.

Zach doesn't see Seven to be as big a threat as what Tess said and ended up taking a deal with them to revive Zandra.
