Summary

Error is an ancient demon from before the creation of deities. He believes demons are the only proper race and should be the only ones allowed to live.

Physical Description

Error looks like a large mass of black goop with a floating head. the only facial feature he has is a large mouth with jagged sharp teeth. A shadowy aura surrounds him at all times where you can hear whispers of past victims he devoured. He reeks of blood, mildew, and rotting bodies.
