Summary

Seven is an amalgamation of several other subjects that died, they all share the same body with different traits form the subjects. Seven themself is a blank personality that formed, they are very aware though.

Physical Description

Seven has a short build and a very thin frame. Due to a lack of natural sunlight, Seven's skin is extremely pale. Seven has short pale yellow hair that ends in pale baby pink highlights, dull silver eyes, and a small pointed nose. Seven is commonly seen wearing a white shirt and white pleated skirt with tall white rain boots.

Seven wears a white mask with a black open smile that covers their face.

Personality

Note:

Seven's body is the host of several different entities. Due to this, this section will focus on primarily SEVEN's personality, and not the others.

Seven typically had an erratic and untrusting personality due to past trauma. As Seven starts to heal from the said trauma, they develop a courageous and caring personality, and a love for adventure like Tess had. The have a strong sense of justice and want to help the innocent who are being controlled and harmed.

Backstory

Seven was created when all of the children from the demon blood experiment merged with Eclipse's body, Seven started off as blank slate inside their mind space and later started forming their own thoughts and emotions.

When Seven was little, she had vampire bat DNA mixed into her human DNA causing her to gain the features and instincts of vampire bats, (Wings, fangs, echolocation, being nocturnal) most of her physical bat features come out voluntarily though.

Main Story

Seven was first seen when Mattie ended up in the lab after falling through a portal, they introduced themselves as Eclipse

Abilities

Precognition

Due to Eclipse's influence, Seven developed a strong ability for seeing the future. After Eclipse and the others leave Seven's body, Seven can only see random things in the distant future.

Flight

Due to the animal experiments, Seven has the ability to fly like a bat. Their wings seem to melt into their back when not in use, this allows the wings to heal and regenerate.

Night Vision/Echolocation

Due to her light colored eyes, Seven is able to see in darkness. Her bat related abilities allow her to use echolocation as well.
