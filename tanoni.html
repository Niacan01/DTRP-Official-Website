Summary

Physical Description

As a Teenager

Tanoni has a height of 5' 6" and a thin build, she has long yellow hair and blue eyes with ivory skin. She wears a school uniform at all times complete with a blue bow in her hair.

As a Child

Tanoni has a height of 4' 2" and a normal build, her shorter yellow hair is tied into two high pig tails with blue beaded hair ties, due to her bangs, her eyes sometimes get covered. She wears a large light blue sweater and brown pants.

Personality

Tanoni tends to come off as shy when first meeting people, eventually she will show herself to be pretty adventurous and brave, she just prefers being soft spoken.

Backstory

Tanoni was the second child born to her unnamed parents, her older sister being Natsuki. When she was still a toddler, her mother was murdered by her father when he was drunk, this effected Tanoni greatly and is why she tends to be more quiet.

Main Story

The gang met Tanoni when Tesseract accidentally teleported them to an unknown dimension.
