Summary

Nicholas is a minor character that is first mentioned during one of the adventures in the post-split up arc. He is the All Seer of the Divided Cities.

Physical Description

Normal

Nicholas is 9 feet tall with light orange skin, light brown eyes, and a circular birthmark around his right eye. He wears black to dark red robes that cover his eyes. He also has a matching hoodie that connects to the robes.

In his other form, he is 12 feet tall with a black body with bubbling skin, dark mist, and multiple red eyes. His robes are mostly visible in this form.

Disguised as Lenti

A tall, conventionally attractive, female demon.

Personality

Nicholas is sweet, patient, and protective. He does not like being weak and suffers with depression due to him being responsible for some of his friends and others deaths. He is very lonely most of the time and does not wish to marry an Oracle in order to not have to suffer watching the Oracle's death in childbirth. When Nicholas gets angry he tends to not listen very well. He prefers to keep his friends close.

Backstory

Nicholas was born to Aethel and Lorelei. Lorelei passed away after Nicholas was born and Aethel eventually left to find something to take down his older brother, Lucius.

Lucius abused Nicholas for about a decade with Aethel occasionally popping in. Eventually Aethel took down his brother and took over as All Seer, freeing the cities from his brother's reign of terror.

With his father back, Nicholas was able to grow up safely and later become All Seer. He made and lost a lot of friends including his adoptive daughter.
