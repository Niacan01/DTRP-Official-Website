Summary

Physical Description

Mattie has a short build with violet eyes,long messy redish-brown hair and peach skin. She wears colorful casual clothing with a purple orb bracelet for her EA and an identical teleportation necklace to Zach's.

Personality

Forgiving, childish, curious, not the type to like battle, expressive, and gets jealous of others frequently.

Backstory

Mattie lived with her rich parents in Pennsylvania until government agents attempted to get her and her mom into a lab because of their ability (EA).

Worried, they sent Mattie away to Oregon. When she turned 5, her parents hired someone to take her to the forest, and erase her memory. She only got kept the memories of her name, age, and birthdate.

They erased her memory as a fail-safe in case she got captured by agents and they interrogated her and hurt her. She lived alone in the forest and when she was 13, she met Tess and the others and became friends with them, going on adventures, and more, eventually meeting her girlfriend, Ruby.

Main Story

Mattie met the dt group in her forest. After spending the day there with them, she decided to fully join their group and travel to different dimensions.

Abilities

  • Extended hearing
  • Extended sense of smell
  • Extended speed and strength
  • Enhanced durability and ability to crack and explode structures with body (EA)

Relationships

Ruby (Girlfriend)

Mattie met Ruby when the DT group traveled to the Shadow Realm, they got along due to their needs for adventure but were separated when the DT group had to leave.

They eventually met again at the School Dimension and officially became friends, causing Ruby to agree to join the DT group.

While at the spa dimension, Ruby followed Zach after he kidnapped Mattie to try and save her. Ruby succeeded, and brought Mattie back.

When the group went to the Snowy dimension, Ruby and Mattie played truth or dare, this led to them confessing their feelings and officially ending up together.

Mattie was devastated when Ruby was killed during Lily's genocide, needless to say they were both happy to be revived.

Zach (best friend)

Mattie was kidnapped by Zach a few times but eventually Zach was redeemed. They now both get along pretty good and are best friends.

Tess

•Mattie likes Tess a lot, but at the same time gets very annoyed with her but is too nice to show it.

Mattie met Tess in the forest after it was revealed that Tess was spying on the others, they got along since Tess seemed pretty cool.

Mattie showed concern for Tess when she nearly died in the Backrooms arc, and was relieved when she ended up being ok.

When the groups split, Tess took Mattie as one of the members of her group, Mattie was trained by Tess there for a few months.

After the training Mattie ended up living at Tess's house with Zach, they went on multiple more adventures there.
