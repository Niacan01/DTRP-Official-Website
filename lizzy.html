Physical Description

In her shadow form, Lizzy has a thin frame with paper white skin and long white hair with black gradient highlights. She keeps her hair tied up into pig tails, eventually she traded this in for shorter hair once she started following Lily around on quests. She has large black eyes and a button nose. In her human form, she has similar features but is taller and has light blonde hair with soft blue eyes.

Personality

Lizzy has a generally cheerful and happy disposition. She likes to try and spread joy to others and it can sometimes be overwhelming. When needed, she can be serious and calculated.

Backstory

Lizzy was born in the 1940's, she grew up being raised by highly abusive parents which she later found out were her aunt and uncle. Eventually she was able to leave with her other aunt and cousins and move to New York.

There she made some friends at a nearby church which was later revealed to be a cult. Her and the others around her age were killed in the 1940 Church Murders which was part of a series of murders committed by the church leader as a sacrifice to Error.

After Lizzy was killed, she was revived in the Shadow World, the church leader imprisoned her in an office to keep her from stopping Error

Main Story

Eighty years after her imprisonment, Lizzy managed to escape the office and explore the ruins of the church. She suffered extreme amnesia and was unable to remember anything except her nickname.

Lizzy eventually met Ben, another victim of the murders. They eventually joined together as a team and met Ruby. She took them both to the Rebuilt City where they met Lizzy's cousins who helped her regain several memories, including that she was a natural born witch

Abilities

Advanced Witchcraft

While Lizzy took a considerable amount of time to learn the basics, she eventually became a highly skilled witch.

  • Elemental manipulation
  • Flight
  • Precognition
  • Astral projection
  • Energy manipulation
  • Extrasensory Perception

Advanced Swordfighting

Like several other members of her family, Lizzy was a born swordfighter. She ended up crafting her own set of dual swords.

Relationships

Ben - Ex-boyfriend

Before the murder events, Lizzy was aware of Bens existence at the school, but had no intentions of ever speaking to him.

When Lizzy escaped the office, Ben was the first passive entity she met, she was excited to have a friend but he was more indifferent due to her overwhelmingly positive disposition. They eventually did become best friends though.

Lizzy and Ben ended up gaining mutual feelings for each other and Ben ended up confessing them around the time they had to battle Sun. After Sun was defeated and frozen, Lizzy told Ben she accepted his confession.

Helen - Really close friend/One sided crush (on Helen's side)

Before the murder events, Lizzy was best friends with Helen at school and would try to help Helen if she was being harassed.

After escaping, Lizzy eventually found out Helen had been murdered as well and taken to Error. Lizzy was possessed several times and it was believed Helen was the one doing it.

Lizzy and the others eventually ended up getting Helen away from Error. After preforming a memory spell, Helen recovered and joined Lizzy.

Ruby - Really close friend

Lily - Best friend - Love interest (only Smile currently feels the same)

Lizzy met Lily when they first traveled to the Shadow Realm. Lizzy seemed to have an instant liking to Lily and talked to them a lot, Lizzy eventually joined their team after meeting them again at The School.
