Summary

Tesseract is the self appointed leader of the DT Group, she is often overconfident and can be very annoying at times. She also has a fierce hatred for the deity race.

Physical Description

Tess has skin which seems to vary between pale to tan, and long neon yellow hair that ends in hot pink gradient highlights. She has electric blue eyes with her right one being covered by an eye patch and her side swept bangs. She wears white coveralls with hot pink accents and a beanie with the same color scheme.

Personality

Tess is overconfident as times and indifferent at others, she sometimes gains a superiority complex due to her deity genetics.

Backstory

Tesseract was born in a large house far away from populated areas. Tess's parents kept her and her little sister, Eclipse, isolated from other humans due to them realizing that they were being hunted.

As she grew up, Tesseract became restless with a want to see the world. She would also have to deal with nightmares that stemmed from Eclipse accidentally leaking her visions to Tess, this caused Tess to become unstable. Eventually, this led to Tess creating a house fire which killed her parents and left Eclipse at the brink of death. Tess was then kidnapped by the Assassins Guild.

Main Story

Tesseract was first met observing Mattie and the other DT members from behind a stack of books, she accidentally knocked them over revealing her location to the others.

Abilities

Portal Creation

One of Tess's first shown powers was her portal creation ability, she can create portals to various locations and dimensions at any given point. She started off being weak with he ability, only being able to make around three a day. Eventually she got to the point she can make an unlimited number with minimal effort.

Tess's portals are generally various shades of translucent blue, you can see faint images of what the other side is. Exceptions are portals to more hidden realms, such as the deity realm (white), or the writers portals. (All of which reflect their writer)

Tess first learned the ability as a toddler, she would focus and be able to see weak point in space and time, she would then open and distort them making small portals for her to reach through. As she got older and started rebelling against the Assassins Guild, her portal abilities fully reawakened and she honed them.
